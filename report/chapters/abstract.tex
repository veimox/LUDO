\begin{abstract}
To complete a LUDO tournament an artificial intelligence has been developed.
A LUDO player with an Artificial Neural Network (ANN) that is evolved with a Genetic Algorithm (GA) has been desined and implemented for this task. 
To determine the skills of the player, a LUDO tournament is carried out with other players as a Q-learning with GA or a weighted input player.
\end{abstract}
